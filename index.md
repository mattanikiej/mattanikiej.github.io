### [Defense Quality: A New Way of Measuring Defensive Impact in the NHL](defense.md)
A comprehensive analysis introducing Defense Quality, a new defensive metric that focuses on scoring chance suppression rather than just goals. This stat addresses gaps in existing defensive metrics, and provides a more nuanced view of defensive impact that complements traditional metrics like Corsi and xG models.
